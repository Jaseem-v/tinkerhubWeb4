What are TinkerHub Campus Communities?

Tinkerhub campus communities is a tinkerhub programme encouraging a tech learning culture on campuses so that students can grow their abilities and further their careers. Campus communities will help college students who don't think they're getting enough exposure to technology or don't have the ideal environment to study it.

Why Tinkerhub Campus Communities?

The fourth industrial revolution is on its way, and talents will be everything. Tinkerhub is dedicated to assisting our student community in upskilling and contributing to the community's progress through the learning process. Tinkerhub's programmes are all oriented on peer-to-peer learning, and we're dedicated to helping our members learn about new technologies, become industry-ready, and, most importantly, network and make new contacts.
